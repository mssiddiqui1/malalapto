Volunteering In Public Schools

V.I.P.S. — Every bit of time you give makes a huge difference for our kids.

Get Involved

Volunteer with MYE PTO

Malala Elementary PTO could not do all of the amazing things it does without the dedicated group of volunteers who are so generous with their time and efforts.

Involvement with the PTO is a wonderful way to get to know the school, staff, students and other parents.

Background Check Required: To be eligible to volunteer you must complete the required FBISD Criminal background check. This applies to parents & community volunteers, field trips, class parties, and chaperones.
Complete FBISD Background Check

Why Volunteer?

  • ✓ Get to know the school and staff
  • ✓ Connect with other parents
  • ✓ Directly impact students' experience
  • ✓ Short-term & long-term opportunities
  • ✓ Both in-school & outside-school roles
