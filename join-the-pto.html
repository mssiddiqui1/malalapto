Join the MYE PTO

Membership is $10 per child. Together we make MYE better!

2024–2025 Membership

Why Join?

The Malala Elementary PTO is a 501(c)(3) parent teacher organization committed to creating and maintaining positive interactions between the school, parents, and community.

We provide financial support for a variety of programs and activities designed to enrich student academics and the overall school experience. We also show appreciation for the school staff throughout the year.

Volunteering is not a requirement of our members, but we encourage every parent or guardian to join the PTO and donate.
